## Phase 1: Technical Foundation (Weeks 4–6 Out)
- [ ] Three financial statement walk-through (out loud, under 90 sec)
- [ ] $10 depreciation change through all 3 statements
- [ ] FCF calculation: unlevered and levered
- [ ] Trading comps walk-through end to end
- [ ] Precedent transactions vs. comps (why multiples differ)
- [ ] DCF walk-through: FCF → terminal value → WACC → equity value per share
- [ ] LBO concept: leverage, returns, ideal candidate
- [ ] M&A: accretion/dilution logic without a calculator

## Phase 2: Technical Drilling (Weeks 2–3 Out)
- [ ] Each category answered out loud without notes (60–90 sec)
- [ ] At least one full 30-min timed mock technical session
- [ ] Weakest category identified and drilled separately
- [ ] Follow-up questions practiced for each category

## Phase 3: Behavioral Prep (Weeks 2–3 Out, Parallel)
- [ ] Story bank: 5–8 real experiences documented (STAR-L format)
- [ ] "Walk me through your resume" under 2 minutes with clear arc
- [ ] "Why investment banking" has specific, defensible reasons
- [ ] "Why this bank" has bank-specific research (recent deals, group info)
- [ ] Pressure story ready: specific action + quantified result
- [ ] Failure story ready: clear ownership + lesson
- [ ] 1 current market/deal discussion topic prepared per target group

## Phase 4: Superday Simulation (Week 1 Out)
- [ ] Day 7: Self-assessment complete, gaps identified
- [ ] Day 6: Weakest technical areas drilled clean
- [ ] Day 5: Story bank refined, all stories under 90 sec
- [ ] Day 4: 2 mixed mock sessions (behavioral + technical)
- [ ] Day 3: Bank/group research complete, 3 questions per interviewer
- [ ] Day 2: Full superday simulation (4 back-to-back sessions)
- [ ] Day 1: Light review only, logistics confirmed, attire ready
- [ ] Day 0: Arrive 10–15 min early, lead with warmth, close with specific question
