Valuation questions usually test whether you know the tools and when each tool is useful.

## The core set

- What are the main valuation methodologies?
- Why do precedent transactions usually trade higher than public comps?
- When would a DCF matter more?
- Why is valuation a range and not one number?

## The right framing

The strongest answers compare methods, explain tradeoffs, and show why no single output should be taken in isolation.

## A simple practice rule

For every valuation concept, be ready to explain:

- what it measures
- what drives it
- when it becomes less useful
