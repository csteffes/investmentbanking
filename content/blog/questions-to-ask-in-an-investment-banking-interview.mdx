Closing questions matter because they show how you think when the formal interview is almost over.

## Better questions to ask

- What separates candidates who ramp quickly from those who do not?
- What kinds of live situations teach the most in this group?
- How has the market changed the work mix over the last year?

## Questions to avoid

- anything answered by the website
- questions that focus only on lifestyle
- questions so broad that they sound automatic

## The best test

Ask yourself: would a banker think this question came from someone who actually wants to understand the work?

If yes, keep it. If not, tighten it.
