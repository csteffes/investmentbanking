Behavioral questions carry more weight than many candidates expect because they shape how every technical answer is heard.

## Questions to prepare first

- Walk me through your resume.
- Why investment banking?
- Why this bank?
- Tell me about a time you handled pressure.
- Tell me about a failure.

## What strong answers have in common

- they get to the point quickly
- they use real details
- they stay easy to defend under follow-up

## What to cut

- long setup
- vague leadership claims
- canned lines that could fit any candidate
