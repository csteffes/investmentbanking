## UTM Convention for Campus Outreach Links

When sharing Superday AI links in campus outreach, finance club newsletters, or alumni communications, use UTM parameters to track source:

```
https://superday.ai/blog/[slug]/?utm_source=[school-or-club-name]&utm_medium=campus-partnership&utm_campaign=ib-recruiting-[year]
```

**Examples:**
- `https://superday.ai/blog/investment-banking-interview-prep-roadmap/?utm_source=wharton-finance-club&utm_medium=campus-partnership&utm_campaign=ib-recruiting-2026`
- `https://superday.ai/blog/investment-banking-superday-prep-7-day-plan/?utm_source=lse-alumni-network&utm_medium=newsletter&utm_campaign=ib-recruiting-2026`

Track in GA4 under **Acquisition → Traffic acquisition** filtered by utm_source.
